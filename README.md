# Red Crew - Coast Guard SES Timeline

An interactive timeline documenting the U.S. Coast Guard's Surface Effect Ship (SES) program during the 1980s War on Drugs.

## About

This project tells the story of the Coast Guard's elite SES Division based in Key West, Florida, featuring the intertwined careers of LT Jim Howe and RADM Wayne Justice during the height of drug interdiction operations in the Caribbean.

## View the Timeline

Open `index.html` in your browser to explore the interactive timeline.

## Key Personnel

- **LT Jim Howe** - Executive Officer of "Red Crew," author of *Red Crew: Fighting the War on Drugs with Reagan's Coast Guard*
- **RADM Wayne Justice** - Commanding Officer of USCGC SHEARWATER (WSES-3) and multiple other commands

## The SES Fleet

Three Surface Effect Ships operated by four rotating crews (Red, Blue, Green, Gold):
- USCGC SEA HAWK (WSES-2)
- USCGC SHEARWATER (WSES-3)
- USCGC PETREL (WSES-4)

## Historical Period

This timeline covers the establishment of the SES Division in November 1982 through its transition in the late 1980s, documenting one of the most intense periods of maritime drug interdiction in U.S. history.
